Interpretation

The place that attracts and sends the most flows is definitely Germany. This is due to the fact that Germany is a very large company that depends on natural gas as it does not have many other homegrown electricity sources, it also most likely has a large amount of natural gas pipeline infrastructure which makes it also a good place to transport natural gas to neighboring countries. Generally, the direction of flow is that it goes towards the middle of the map, being Europe. I believe this is due to the fact that natural gas resources are not as strong in the middle of Europe as they have been exhausted or are just in general not very abundant, this makes Europe dependent on natural gas from other sources, many of which are outside of Europe or are on the fringes of Europe.
